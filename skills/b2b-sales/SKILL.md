# B2B Sales Playbook for Elevare Health

Comprehensive guide to B2B partnership development for men's telehealth services.

## Target Markets

### Primary: Wellness Clinics
- **Profile:** Independent clinics offering hormone optimization, functional medicine, anti-aging
- **Pain Points:** Patient acquisition, admin burden, limited telehealth capabilities
- **Value Prop:** White-label platform, revenue share, zero platform fees

### Secondary: Compounding Pharmacies
- **Profile:** Pharmacies that compound TRT, peptides, custom formulations
- **Pain Points:** Need patient referrals, want provider relationships
- **Value Prop:** Preferred partner status, steady referral stream

## Outreach Strategy

### Cold Email Sequence

**Email 1: Introduction (Day 0)**
- Subject: Partnership opportunity for {{company}}
- Hook: Reference specific service they offer
- Value: Brief revenue share mention
- CTA: 15-minute call

**Email 2: Value-Add (Day 3)**
- Subject: Re: Partnership opportunity
- Provide useful insight about industry
- Mention case study results
- CTA: Quick reply

**Email 3: Social Proof (Day 7)**
- Subject: How {{similar_company}} increased revenue 40%
- Share specific case study
- Include metrics
- CTA: Book a call link

**Email 4: Breakup (Day 14)**
- Subject: Closing the loop
- Acknowledge they're busy
- Leave door open
- Final CTA

### Phone Outreach

**Best Times:**
- Tuesday-Thursday
- 10 AM - 12 PM local time
- 2 PM - 4 PM local time

**Voicemail Script:**
> "Hi {{first_name}}, this is Paul from Elevare Health. I'm reaching out because we partner with clinics like {{company}} to help them add telehealth capabilities and generate additional revenue with zero platform fees. I'd love to share how we helped [similar clinic] add $12K in monthly revenue. My number is [number]. Again, it's Paul from Elevare. Thanks!"

### LinkedIn Strategy

1. Connect with decision makers
2. Engage with their content first
3. Wait 3-5 days before messaging
4. Reference something specific from their profile
5. Soft CTA (resource, insight, question)

## Lead Scoring Framework

**Total Score: 0-100**

### Fit Score (50 points)
- Services align with Elevare: 0-20
- Company size in range: 0-15
- Geographic fit: 0-10
- Growth signals: 0-5

### Intent Score (30 points)
- No current telehealth: +15
- Recent expansion/hiring: +10
- Technology adoption: +5

### Accessibility (20 points)
- Decision maker identified: +10
- Direct contact found: +5
- LinkedIn active: +5

**Priority Tiers:**
- 80-100: Hot (contact within 24h)
- 60-79: Warm (contact within 1 week)
- 40-59: Nurture (add to sequence)
- <40: Deprioritize

## Objection Handling

### "We already have a telehealth solution"
> "That's great - may I ask which platform you're using? We often work alongside existing solutions. Our partners typically see us as complementary because we bring additional patients specifically seeking hormone optimization. Would it be worth a quick conversation to see if there's a fit?"

### "We're too small"
> "Actually, that's often an advantage. Smaller practices can move faster and capture market share before larger competitors. Our smallest partner has just 3 providers and generates $8K/month from Elevare patients. What's holding you back from growth right now?"

### "What's the catch with zero platform fees?"
> "Fair question. We make money when you make money - it's a revenue share model. We handle patient acquisition, the platform, and support. You provide the clinical expertise. It aligns our incentives. Would you like to see the full partnership terms?"

### "Send me information"
> "Absolutely. What's most important to you - the revenue model, the clinical workflow, or the patient experience? I want to make sure I send you the most relevant information."
