# Brand wizard — onboarding gate

Per [`ZEUS_OUTREACH_PLAN.md`](ZEUS_OUTREACH_PLAN.md) §11: branding **starts the stack**; completed onboarding **unlocks** templates for posts, email, and influencer outreach.

## Flow (v1)

1. **Chat-style or stepped UI** — logo upload, voice summary, taboos, colors.
2. **Writes** → **`BrandProfiles`** (Airtable): Product link, Voice summary, Taboos, Logo, Guidelines URL.
3. **Onboarding completed** checkbox → Mission Control enables Publish templates, email snippets, UGC outreach shells.

## Zeus Web

Route **`/brand/wizard`** — dedicated wizard shell (stub → full form). **`/brand`** remains kit overview.

## Compliance

Regulated brands: optional **counsel PDF** attachment + **Compliance copilot** flag (see **`V2_BACKLOG_PICKS.md`**) before auto-post.
